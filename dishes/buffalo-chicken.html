Buffalo Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • Ranch Seasoning
  • 12 oz. hot sauce
  • 2 lbs. chicken breasts
  • 4 Tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts into bottom of a large slow cooker.
  2. Cut butter into chunks and place ontop of chicken.
  3. Add ranch seasoning and hot sauce overtop of chicken.
  4. Close slow cooker and cook on low heat for 4 hours. Shred chicken and serve.
