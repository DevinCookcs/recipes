Slow Cooker Meatballs

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 lb. italian sausage
  • 1 large egg (beaten)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup parmesan
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 26 oz. jar marinara sauce
  • 12 oz. spaghetti

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, sausage, egg, breadcrumbs, parmesan, onion powder, garlic powder and salt to bowl.
  2. Divide mixture into equal portions and form into balls.
  3. Place meatballs into slow cooker and pour marinara over top.
  4. Close slow cooker and cook low for ~5 hours.
  5. Near end of meatballs cook time prepare spaghetti according to packaging and serve.
